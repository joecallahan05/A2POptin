ECS SMS Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Chickasaw Nation (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and discloses personal information when you opt in to receive text message notifications (SMS) via our Enhanced Citizen Services (ECS) platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone Number: Collected when you opt in via our web form.
  • Name and Email: May be collected if you provide them in the ECS form.
  • Consent Timestamp: Date and time you opted in.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you informational SMS messages, including form confirmations, appointment reminders, status updates, and service notifications.
  • To maintain records of your opt‑in status and preferences.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements related to SMS messaging.

3. SMS Message Details

  • Message Origin: All messages originate from one of our approved ECS sender numbers.
  • Frequency: You may receive up to 5 messages per month, depending on your interactions.
  • Message & Data Rates: Standard carrier rates apply.

4. Opt‑Out & Help

You can unsubscribe from all ECS SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. To receive more information, reply HELP or contact us at (580)757‑9082.

5. Data Retention & Security

We retain your phone number, consent timestamp, and messaging history for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. We employ industry‑standard safeguards to protect your data.

6. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With Twilio, our A2P 10DLC messaging provider, for the purpose of delivering SMS.
  • To comply with legal requests, court orders, or government investigations.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last Updated” date below indicates when changes were made.
